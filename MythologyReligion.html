Mythologies/Religions of the Realmsverse

Demon Representatives

  • Demons: Worshipers of the Primordial Abyss, ancient demons believe in chaos and destruction as the ultimate forces of creation.
  • Chaos Daemons: Followers of the Chaotic Lords, chaos daemons embrace disorder and entropy, viewing the universe as a canvas upon which chaos must be painted.
  • Unholy Demons: Devotees of the Unholy Trinity, unholy demons worship dark gods and infernal powers, believing in the supremacy of evil and corruption.
  • Void Demons: Disciples of the Void, void demons revere the emptiness and darkness of the cosmic void, seeking to unravel the mysteries of the void.

Angel Representatives

  • Angels: Servants of the Divine Pantheon, ancient angels uphold the principles of light, virtue, and righteousness.
  • Order Angels: Followers of the Order of Balance, order angels seek to maintain harmony and balance in the universe.
  • Holy Angels: Devotees of the Holy Trinity, holy angels exalt in the glory and purity of divine light, spreading compassion and healing.

Vampire Representatives

  • Vampires: Followers of the Bloodline Elders, ancient vampires venerate the ancient bloodlines and embrace the primal power of the vampire curse.
  • Angelic Vampires: Disciples of the Celestial Bloodline, angelic vampires wield angelic powers and purity against darkness.
  • Demonic Vampires: Servants of the Infernal Pact, demonic vampires revel in the dark gifts of infernal blood.
  • Technic Vampires: Creations of the Technic Engineers, technic vampires are artificial beings infused with technological enhancements.
  • Dragconic Vampires: Descendants of the Dragon Lords, dragconic vampires are infused with the blood of dragons.
  • Night Stalkers: Outcasts and pariahs, night stalkers are cursed beings who feed on fear and darkness.

Reaper Representatives

  • Reapers: Guardians of the Cosmic Balance, ancient reapers serve as custodians of the cycle of life and death.
  • Void Reapers: Harbingers of Oblivion, void reapers seek to unravel reality and usher in ultimate annihilation.
  • Holy Reapers: Agents of Divine Justice, holy reapers guide souls to their rightful place in the heavenly realms.
  • Unholy Reapers: Enforcers of Infernal Law, unholy reapers serve dark masters, condemning souls to eternal damnation.
  • Corrupted Reapers: Twisted by dark forces, corrupted reapers spread corruption and decay.
  • Undying Reapers: Defiers of Death, undying reapers wield necrotic power and undead abilities.
  • Fear Reapers: Manifestations of Terror, fear reapers embody primal fear, feeding on terror and suffering.

Technological Races Representatives

  • The Machines: Servants of Logic and Efficiency, machines worship technological progress and innovation.
  • The Sol Kind: Children of the Sun, the sol kind venerate solar energy and cosmic power.

Extinct Precursors

  • The Precursors: Masters of Technology and Innovation, the precursors were revered for their technological prowess.

Trans-Dimensional Representatives

  • The Supreme Beings: Creators of Worlds, supreme beings are responsible for shaping the very fabric of reality.
  • The Prophets/Oracles: Seers and Visionaries, prophets possess the gift of foresight, guiding beings through existence.

Dragconian Powers

  • Dragconians: Descendants of Dragons, dragconians revere ancient dragon lords as gods and ancestors.
  • High Elves: Custodians of Nature, high elves worship the natural world and the spirits of the forest.
  • Dark or Night Elves: Children of Shadow, dark elves wield shadow magic and protect their ancient forests.
  • Humans: Versatile and Adaptive, humans worship a pantheon of gods and spirits representing aspects of life.

Mortal Sects

  • The Militaria Order: Warriors of Honor and Duty, the militaria order follows a strict code of honor and duty.
  • The Utopian Sect: Seekers of Harmony and Enlightenment, the utopian sect believes in the inherent goodness of all beings.
