Dragconia

Dragconians

Born of the union between humans and dragons, the Dragconians embody the fusion of mortal and draconic bloodlines. They revere and live in harmony with the noble Domestic dragons, forming deep bonds forged in mutual respect and understanding.

High Elves

Dwellers of the majestic High Forest of G'lven, the High Elves exude an air of haughty superiority, their pride matched only by their innate magical prowess. Their secluded realm teems with ancient wisdom and arcane secrets, guarded jealously by its enigmatic inhabitants.

Dark or Night Elves

In the shadowy recesses of the Dark Land, the Night Elves weave their intricate webs of intrigue and deceit. Masters of shadow magic and subterfuge, they navigate the treacherous depths of their domain with cunning and guile, ever wary of outside interference.

Dwarves

Within the rugged confines of the H'thrd Mountain Range, the stout-hearted Dwarves craft their legendary brews amidst the echoing halls of their mountain strongholds. Renowned for their craftsmanship and resilience, they are a proud and industrious race, fiercely loyal to their kin and their traditions.

Humans

In the frigid expanses of the North and South Wastelands, humans eke out a harsh existence amidst the biting cold and relentless snowstorms. Believed to be the supposed Vikings of the Cold, they possess a rugged tenacity and fierce spirit, tempered by the harsh realities of their unforgiving environment.

Dragon Variants

From the skies to the depths below, Dragconia is home to a myriad of dragon variants, each with its own unique characteristics and abilities:

  • Wyverns: Agile and cunning hunters of the skies, wyverns soar through the clouds with unmatched grace and agility.
  • Mud Drakes: Burrowers of the earth, mud drakes traverse the labyrinthine tunnels beneath Dragconia's surface, their keen senses attuned to the shifting sands and rocky terrain.
  • The Old Ones: Ancient and wise beyond measure, the Old Ones are revered as the progenitors of dragonkind, their wisdom sought by mortals and immortals alike.
  • The Rising Suns: Radiant beings of celestial fire, the Rising Suns blaze a trail across the heavens, their golden wings igniting the sky with their divine presence.
  • The Earth Demons: Guardians of the subterranean realms, earth demons embody the raw power of the earth itself, their mighty forms forged from the very bedrock of Dragconia.
  • The Shadow Drakes: Masters of darkness and illusion, shadow drakes stalk the night with silent grace, their obsidian scales blending seamlessly with the shadows.
  • The River Dragons: Navigators of the vast riverways that crisscross Dragconia, river dragons ply the waters with ease, their sinuous forms cutting through the currents like knives through silk.
  • The Fire Demons: Incarnations of infernal flame, fire demons wield the power of combustion and destruction, their fiery breath laying waste to all who oppose them.
  • The Blood Titans: Colossal behemoths of crimson scale and iron will, blood titans command the respect and fear of all who behold their towering forms.
  • The Air Demons: Lords of the stormy skies, air demons ride the winds with wild abandon, their howling roars echoing across Dragconia's vast expanses.
  • The Ocean Drakes: Masters of the boundless seas, ocean drakes reign over the depths with regal majesty, their shimmering scales reflecting the endless expanse of the ocean's depths.
  • The Fake Deaths: Illusory tricksters of the skies, fake deaths weave intricate webs of deception, luring unsuspecting prey into their carefully laid traps.
  • The Water Demons: Guardians of the aqueous realms, water demons command the tides and currents with a flick of their serpentine tails, their aquatic domains teeming with life and mystery.
  • The Flower Dragons: Guardians of nature's beauty, flower dragons roam Dragconia's verdant landscapes, their delicate wings alight with the colors of a thousand blossoms.
