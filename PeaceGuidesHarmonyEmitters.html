Using Harmony Emitters

To activate a Harmony Emitter, locate the power switch or activation button typically located on the device's handle or casing. Press or toggle the switch to power on the emitter.

3. Pointing the Emitter

Aim the Harmony Emitter towards the intended targets, ensuring that the emitter is pointed in their direction for optimal effect. Maintain a steady grip on the device to prevent accidental misalignment during operation.

4. Activating the Emission

Once aimed at the target, activate the emission mechanism of the Harmony Emitter to release the soothing frequencies and harmonious vibrations into the surrounding environment. Depending on the model, this may involve pressing a designated emission button or trigger.

5. Monitoring Response

Observe the reactions of the target audience to gauge the effectiveness of the Harmony Emitter. Look for signs of relaxation, decreased aggression, and increased receptivity to communication and negotiation.
