The Angels

The Supreme One

Revered as the pinnacle of angelic hierarchy, the Supreme One reigns as the undisputed leader of all Angelkind. Radiating boundless divine power and wisdom, they guide their celestial brethren with benevolence and grace, ensuring harmony and order across the celestial realms.

Ancient Angels

Beings of timeless wisdom and ancient lineage, the Ancient Angels stand as guardians of celestial knowledge and cosmic balance. With their ageless wisdom, they serve as custodians of celestial lore, preserving the sacred teachings passed down through eons.

High-Blood Angels

Descendants of the most noble celestial lineage, the High-Blood Angels embody purity and virtue, their bloodline steeped in divine grace and majesty. Revered for their unwavering commitment to righteousness, they stand as beacons of celestial glory and honor.

Royal Angels

Nobility among Angelkind, the Royal Angels rule over celestial realms with regal bearing and divine authority. Their celestial courts are renowned for their splendor and magnificence, as they preside over matters of cosmic significance with grace and wisdom.

Angels

The heart and soul of angelic legions, these ethereal beings serve as messengers and guardians of the divine realm. With their celestial grace and unwavering devotion, they carry out the will of their heavenly overseers, spreading light and hope across the cosmos.

Lesser Angels

Though humble in stature, these celestial beings possess boundless potential and unwavering dedication to the celestial cause. As foot soldiers of divine justice, they stand ready to defend the celestial realms against the forces of darkness and corruption.

Ancient Order Angels

Keepers of celestial balance and cosmic harmony, the Ancient Order Angels uphold the sacred tenets of divine law and righteousness. With their timeless vigilance, they safeguard the celestial realms from the encroachment of chaos and discord.

High-Blood Order Angels

Descendants of celestial nobility, the High-Blood Order Angels embody the ideals of virtue and righteousness, their divine heritage guiding them in their sacred duty to uphold cosmic order. With their unwavering resolve, they stand as bastions of celestial purity and grace.

Royal Order Angels

Nobility among the celestial hierarchy, the Royal Order Angels command respect and admiration among their brethren, their regal bearing and divine authority ensuring order and harmony across the celestial realms.

Order Angels

Paragons of celestial discipline and virtue, the Order Angels serve as the enforcers of divine law and righteousness. With their unwavering commitment to order and harmony, they stand as pillars of strength and stability in the face of cosmic upheaval.

Lesser Order Angels

Humble servants of celestial order, these ethereal beings embody the virtues of humility and dedication, their unwavering loyalty to the celestial cause guiding them in their sacred duty to uphold cosmic balance.

Ancient Holy Angels

Archetypes of celestial purity and divine grace, the Ancient Holy Angels stand as paragons of righteousness and virtue. With their celestial radiance, they illuminate the heavens with their unwavering light and beauty, inspiring awe and reverence among mortal and celestial alike.
