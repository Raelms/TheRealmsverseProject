Battles and Wars of the Realmsverse

The Siege of the Silver Citadel

  • Duration: 2 weeks
  • Factions: Ancient Angels vs. Royal Demons
  • Losses: Ancient Angels - 40%, Royal Demons - 70%
  • Army Size: Ancient Angels - 15,000, Royal Demons - 20,000

The War of the Shattered Realms

  • Duration: 1 year
  • Factions: The Machines vs. The Sol Kind
  • Losses: Machines - 50%, Sol Kind - 60%
  • Army Size: Machines - 50,000, Sol Kind - 40,000

The Battle of the Blazing Plains

  • Duration: 1 day
  • Factions: Ancient Chaos Daemons vs. Order of the Celestial Sentinels
  • Losses: Chaos Daemons - 80%, Sentinels - 10%
  • Army Size: Chaos Daemons - 25,000, Sentinels - 20,000

The Skirmish at the Shadowed Pass

  • Duration: 3 hours
  • Factions: Society of Shadows vs. Guild of Thieves and Rogues
  • Losses: Minimal (under 5%)
  • Army Size: Shadows - 500, Thieves - 450

The Battle of Infernal Rift

  • Duration: 3 days
  • Factions: Royal Demons vs. Angels
  • Losses: High
  • Details: A demonic incursion through a celestial rift, sealed by angelic forces after heavy fighting.

The War of Blood and Light

  • Duration: 7 years
  • Factions: Vampires vs. Holy Reapers
  • Losses: Moderate
  • Details: A territorial and vendetta-driven conflict, ending with a fragile peace.

The Battle of Techno Realms

  • Duration: 1 month
  • Factions: Machines vs. Sol Kind
  • Losses: Minimal
  • Details: A short conflict resolved through diplomacy, avoiding significant casualties.

The Siege of Forgotten Citadel

  • Duration: 2 weeks
  • Factions: Undying Reapers vs. Faceless Ones
  • Losses: High
  • Details: A mysterious citadel consumed by an otherworldly cataclysm after intense fighting.

The Battle of Celestial Nexus

  • Duration: 1 day
  • Factions: Supreme Beings vs. Prophets/Oracles
  • Losses: Minimal
  • Details: A brief clash resolved through parley, preserving balance in the Realmsverse.

Battle of the Abyssal Rift

  • Duration: 1 week
  • Factions: Demons, Angels, Reapers
  • Losses: Heavy
  • Details: A struggle for control of a portal to the Abyssal Plane, with significant casualties.

War of the Singularity

  • Duration: 5 years
  • Factions: Machines, Faceless Ones, Sol Kind, Reapers
  • Losses: Devastating
  • Details: A multi-dimensional war over a cosmic artifact, causing widespread destruction.

Battle of the Celestial Gates

  • Duration: 3 days
  • Factions: Angels, Demons, Celestial Sentinels
  • Losses: Significant
  • Details: An assault on celestial gates, with heavy fighting and many gates destroyed.
